StepDub

Using StepDub

  1. Start StepDub.
  2. Tap a shoe icon to try that sound set.
  3. Put the phone in your pocket.
  4. Run around!

Troubleshooting

I don't hear anything!

Have you tried a different shoe? (Hint: If you're not hearing anything for the "ninja stealth" option in the top-left corner, remember: ninjas are very, very quiet.)

If you shake the device, does the line behind the StepDub wordmark at the top of the screen show peaks? If so, check that your sound is turned up loud enough to hear, and that your phone is somewhere on your body where the device can feel your footsteps with as much force as you're shaking it.

If the graph at the top of the screen isn't showing any movement, see the "Support" section below.

I don't see anything!

This might be an issue I haven't encountered that pops up on older older devices. See the "Support" section below for ways you can let me know.

Support

If none of the questions above have solved your problem, you can file an issue on the issue tracker on Github at https://github.com/stuartpb/stepdub/issues, tweet @StepDubApp, or send me an email at stuart@testtrack4.com. Please mention what kind of device you're using, and what version of the OS it's running.
